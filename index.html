How we met

After matching on Bumble on my way back from a friend's bachelor party, Reagan and I first met for drinks at the Broken English Taco Pub in Lincoln Park. I was instantly struck by Reagan's beauty and wanted to know more about my future bride. After much engaging conversation, Reagan and I agreed to attend a pop-punk happy hour at Beauty Bar later that evening. Afterwards, we traveled with a few friends to Bordel; a speakeasy with fantastic cocktails and cabaret. In that very same spot, I would ask for Reagan's hand in marriage on March 12th 2021.
