How we met

After matching on Bumble on my way back from a friend's bachelor party, Reagan and I first met for drinks at the Broken English Taco Pub in Lincoln Park. I was instantly struck by Reagan's beauty and wanted to know more about my future bride. After much engaging conversation, Reagan and I agreed to attend a pop-punk happy hour at Beauty Bar later that evening. Afterwards, we traveled with a few friends to Bordel; a speakeasy with fantastic cocktails and cabaret. In that very same spot, I would ask for Reagan's hand in marriage on March 12th 2021.

Parking

We're recommending most guests use Uber, Lyft, or public transportation as this wedding is going to be lit, fam. However, if you insist on driving and parking at the venue, we're currently deciding one or both parking options below. Please indicate if you are interested in Parking in the RSVP.

Near Venue Self Parking

Colvin House has a select agreement with a near by business for self parking for day of weddings. Cost per parking spot is $15, but needs to be specified up front. Please indicate in your RSVP if you are interested in parking and prefer Self-Parking (in the notes).

Valet Parking

A near-by parking facility can potentially provide valet service for just $20 per vehicle. However, we are leaning away from this service as Self-Parking is closer. Regardless, please indicate in your RSVP if you are interested in parking and prefer Self-Parking (in the notes).
